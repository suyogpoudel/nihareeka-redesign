## About Nihareeka College

Nihareeka College of Management and IT is the **top college in Koshi Province**, dedicated to providing high-quality education in **Science, Technology, Management, Hotel Management, and Humanities**. Since its inception, the college has been an **invitation to learning through both theory and practice**.

The college offers a range of programs with **100% success results** and strong **industry partnerships** that ensure students gain practical experience and career readiness.
